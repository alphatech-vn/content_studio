An AI-powered platform designed for content creators and marketing teams to streamline multi-channel publishing. Content Studio serves as an all-in-one workspace that bridges the gap between creative ideation and final distribution.

Content Studio is a commercial application by AlphaTech. This repository is a public product-information and support hub. It does not contain the application source code.

## Key Features

- **Automated Content Generation:** Leverage AI to quickly draft, ideate, and iterate on marketing copy, blog posts, and social media content.
- **Centralized Asset Library:** Store, organize, and retrieve all your creative assets in one unified workspace.
- **Collaborative Editing Tools:** Work together with your team in real-time to refine content before it goes live.
- **Multi-Channel Publishing:** Bridge the gap between creation and distribution by easily exporting and publishing to various platforms.

## Why it exists

Marketing teams and creators often struggle with fragmented workflows—ideation happens in one tool, asset management in another, and publishing in a third. Content Studio was built to unify these steps, combining the speed of AI generation with the structure of a professional asset library and collaborative environment.

## Product Boundaries

- **Not an open-source project:** This repository exists for documentation, issue tracking, and roadmap visibility. The core application is closed-source.
- **SaaS Platform:** Content Studio is provided as a hosted service.
